PROJECT SCOPE
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

✅ BUILT: CarbonLedger — A decentralized, verified carbon credit marketplace
   where developers tokenize carbon credits as RWAs on Stellar/Soroban,
   corporations purchase using USDC, and irreversibly retire (burn) them
   to claim offsets, with complete provenance on-chain.

SECURITY GUARANTEES
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

✓ Double-Counting Prevention: Serial ranges, overlap detection, DB constraints
✓ Retirement Immutability: No reversal function, permanent on-chain status
✓ Authorization: .require_auth() on all state-changing functions
✓ Data Integrity: Referential integrity, unique constraints, atomic updates
✓ No Private Keys in Code: Environment-based secrets management
